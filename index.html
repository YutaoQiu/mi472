K/DA  is a virtual girl group consisting of four themed versions of League of Legends characters: Ahri, Akali, Evelynn and Kai'Sa. American singers Madison Beer and Jaira Burns and (G)I-dle members Miyeon and Soyeon provide the voices of Evelynn, Kai'Sa, Ahri, and Akali respectively, though the characters have also been voiced by other artists. K/DA was developed by Riot Games, the company behind League of Legends, and unveiled at the 2018 League of Legends World Championship with an augmented reality live performance of their first song, "Pop/Stars". A music video of the song uploaded to YouTube subsequently went viral, surpassing 100 million views in one month, and reaching 400 million views, as well as topping Billboard's World Digital Song Sales chart. This would be followed up with the release of an EP in 2020, entitled "K/DA: ALL OUT" and featured 5 new songs, most notably the title track "More" as it received a music video which has 54 million views as of December 2020

AKALI
Akali's street aesthetic and hard-hitting lyrics inspired a dedicated fandom. She wields a microphone or Kama as a hip-hop ninja, revolutionizing the stale scene with her raw talents.
AHRI
• Ahri is a nine-tailed fox mage in League, and one of the most popular characters in League. In-game, her main weapon is her orb of deception, which she can command to damage enemies and then return to her. She can also heal herself by hurting others, launch 'foxfire' missiles towards her enemies, charm enemies to immobilize them and use her ultimate ability "spirit rush" to rapidly dash.[8] In K/DA, Ahri serves as the leader and founder of K/DA, as well as one of the main vocalists along with Evelynn.[8] Her solo song was 'I'll show you'. According to Miyeon, she started learning more about League and actually played the game as Ahri to better understand her character.[13] Miyeon was invited to visit Riot Headquarters in 2020 along with Soyeon to play League of Legends as their represented characters and skins
evelynn
• Evelynn is an invisible assassin-type Succubus, who appears from her shadow-form using her charm spell to immobilize enemies, followed by quickly assasinating them with her execution ultimate spell.[8] In K/DA, Evelynn serves as one of the main vocalists along with Ahri.[8] Her solo song was "Villain"
kaisa
Kaisa is a burst marksman in-game who can either snipe enemies from afar, or use her ultimate ability to teleport close to them and use another ability to fire missiles from magical cannons floating above her shoulders. She also has a dash ability which can grant her invisibility. The daughter of another champion named Kassadin. After being trapped in a dimension called the "Void", she returned as a killer and binded with living void creatures.[8] In K/DA, Kai'Sa is the main dancer of the group. She also rapped in 'The Baddest' and 'More', and sung in 'Pop/stars' and 'Drum go dum'.[8] Her solo song was "Drum go Dum"
